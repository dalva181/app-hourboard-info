Hourboard iOS Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal data is handled in Hourboard for iOS.

No personal information or app data is collected

All data created and modified within the Hourboard for iOS app is stored locally on your device and is of exclusive access to you. Personal settings within the app are also stored locally on your device and cannot be accessed by anyone but yourself.

Complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act

No personal information or app data is collected, including of children under the age of 13.
